# Medical Schedule Planner

## Overview  
The Medical Schedule Planner is a comprehensive tool designed to assist medical students and professionals in managing their academic and clinical responsibilities efficiently. This project aims to create an organized approach to tracking important milestones and activities throughout their medical education journey.

## Features  
- **Exam Tracking**:  
  Stay updated on upcoming exams with customizable notifications and reminders to keep your study schedule on track.
- **Study Sessions**:  
  Organize your study time with a built-in calendar that allows for scheduling and managing study sessions effectively. Make the most of your study hours by keeping track of topics and resources.
- **Clinical Rotations**:  
  Easily manage and schedule clinical rotations. The planner will help keep track of different rotation schedules, locations, and necessary preparations for clinical encounters.

## Getting Started  
Follow the setup guide to configure the planner according to your specific needs and preferences.  
Whether you’re preparing for exams or managing rotations, the Medical Schedule Planner will streamline your planning process.
